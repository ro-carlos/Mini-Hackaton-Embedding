## Where can I find internships abroad?

### Interships offers with ever-open call

#### [AIESEC](en/mobility/outgoing/internship-abroad.md)

[AIESEC](index.md) is a global organization completely managed by students and
recent graduates from higher education institutions from all over the world.
They have an international platform to offer international internships and
voluntary work. Check all the [opportunities](index.md) AIESEC offers.

[AIESEC Global Talent
presentation](sites/fib/files/documents/mobilitat/aiesec_info_session_gt_it-1.pdf.md)

#### [IAESTE](en/mobility/outgoing/internship-abroad.md)

[IAESTE](index.md) is a global organisation that process international and
paid internships to students of technical specialisation.

#### [Erasmus+](en/mobility/outgoing/internship-abroad.md)

Website to find internships proposed by companies in the Erasmus+ programme:
[http://erasmusintern.org](index.md)

#### [Entrypark](en/mobility/outgoing/internship-abroad.md)

Website for companies who offer interships, some with the option of doing the
degree thesis: [entrypark](careerbook/global.md)

More information about internships abroad in the [International Relations
Bureau of the UPC](sri/ca/estudiantat/mobilitat-estudiants/mobilitat-
destudiantat-de-la-upc/treballar-a-lestranger.md)'s website.

I you find a company to do an internship, you have to get in touch with the
International Relations Area of the FIB by sending a e-mail to  to manage your
mobility.

### Internship offers in academic institutions

#### [Georgia Institute of Technology - United
States](en/mobility/outgoing/internship-abroad.md)

Paid interships with the possibility of carrying out the master's thesis at
the **[Georgia Institute of Technology](index.md)** (Atlanta,GA - United
States).**** Internships are only recommended for master's students. You will
have to stay for one semester at a research lab. A good academic record is
required, as well as a high English level. In addition, you will have to pass
an interview in English with the FIB vicedean of International Relations.
**Call in december.**

#### [École Polytechnique Fédéral de Lausanne -
Switzerland](en/mobility/outgoing/internship-abroad.md)

Summer internships at the [**École Polytechnique Fédérale de
Lausanne**](computer-science/summer-at-epfl.md) (EPFL, Switzerland). They are
directed to both bachelor's and master's students. A good academic record is
required.

#### [Haute École d’Ingénierie et de Gestion du Canton de Vaud -
Switzerland](en/mobility/outgoing/internship-abroad.md)

**HEIG-VD** offers the possibility of realize internships or the TFG/TFM in
its research laboratories. Interns will receive a grant equivalent to free
housing + payment of basic expenses (CHF 400.- / month)

[List of proposed projects](sites/fib/files/documents/mobilitat/heig-vd-
projectes-disponibles.pdf.md) For more details about the project you must
contact the teacher in question.

The students interested in doing the internship should send an e-mail to  with
the title of the project of their interested, the CV and the transcript of
records.

#### [Eidgenössische Technische Hoschschule Zürich -

both bachelor and master students.

[**Amgen Scholars Programme:**](university/eidgenoessische-technische-

[**Robotics Student Fellowship:**](education/robotics-student-

#### [Grenoble Institute of Technology -

internship or a bachelor’s or master’s thesis at its
[laboratories](en/research/laboratories.md). Get in touch with the laboratory
where you want to do the internship directly.

#### [Aalto University - Finland](en/mobility/outgoing/internship-abroad.md)

Summer internships in Aalto Science Institute (Aalto, Espoo). These
internships are dedicated to Bachelor students and Master students, require
good knowledge of the chosen field. In addition, good English level is

## What do I have to do if I already found an internship?

Don't forget to contact the International Relations Office,  .

You will have to fill in the Traineeship Agreement.

If the company is in one of the lands in the Erasmus+ programme (KA131 or
KA171) you can, also, request the [Erasmus+ Traineeship
grant.](sga/ca/Beques/BequesMobilitat.md)

The interships may last from 2 to 12 months per study cycle, leaving out the
months already taken from previous Erasmus+ calls.

In order to guarantee an adequate coverage in case of an accident or
emergency, **the UPC outgoing mobility registration incorporates the mandatory
subscription of an[insurance policy](sri/en/students/students-mobility-
office/outgoings/going-abroad/compulsory-insurance/compulsory-insurance.md)**.
A compulsory insurance payment will be included in the price of the enrolment.
The insurance will cover the whole period of the mobility stay. . When
formalizing the enrollment, the certificate of coverage and contact
information in case of emergency will be available for the student in the
mobility documentation section of the e-Secretaria.
